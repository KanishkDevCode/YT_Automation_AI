Rick's portal gun is calibrated to rip open wormholes in areas with an unusually high concentration of dark matter, but it also leaves behind a residual energy signature that attracts the attention of interdimensional parasites.

In "Rick Potion #9," we see Rick create a multiverse-spanning network using his portal gun. Theoretically, this creates a resonance frequency that echoes through adjacent dimensions, allowing for navigation and communication across vast distances.

Fact 1: Portal gun requires dark matter to function.

Fact 2: Interdimensional parasites are drawn to residual energy signatures.

Conclusion: Given the inherent connection between portal gun usage and interdimensional parasite attraction, it's likely Rick's frequent travel through the multiverse has made him a magnet for these entities.
