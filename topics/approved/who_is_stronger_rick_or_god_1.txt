Who's stronger, Rick Sanchez or God? Sounds crazy, but let's consider this: in the episode where they go to alternate dimensions and meet a bunch of versions of themselves, one version is basically just a cosmic janitor cleaning up after other universes. If that's not a job description for God, I don't know what is.

But Rick's got skills too – he can literally pull reality apart with his portal gun. He's been to the Council of Ricks, where they debate and sometimes destroy entire timelines. That's some next-level problem-solving right there.

So who's stronger? Is it God, who created infinite universes and probably has some sort of divine laundry list for cleaning up after them all? Or is it Rick, who can rip apart reality like it's a cheap knockoff from the Gloopernaughts?

One thing's for sure: if you're ever in a fight with either of these guys, don't bother trying to outsmart them – just make sure your health insurance covers multiverse trauma.
