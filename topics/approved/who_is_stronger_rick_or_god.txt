Imagine a universe where Rick Sanchez isn't the smartest being in existence - and yet, he's constantly trying to outsmart the almighty God himself. Is it possible for anyone to surpass Rick's intellect? We know from "Rick Potion #9" that even the Council of Ricks couldn't keep up with him.

God, on the other hand, is notoriously inconsistent in his powers and motivations - a trickster at best. He's been outwitted by Rick more times than we can count, including that time in Dimension C-137 where he created an entire alternate reality to avoid accountability. 

But don't forget about the "Rick and Morty" episode where God himself becomes the main antagonist - clearly not as all-powerful as he claims. Could it be that his omnipotence is just a facade? If Rick's intelligence is the key to outsmarting God, then what does that say about our own existence?

The answer might surprise you: in a universe governed by chaos and unpredictability, even God isn't immune to being outmaneuvered - as long as there's someone crazy enough to try. Now imagine if Rick ever decided to put his vast knowledge of dark matter to use against the divine...
