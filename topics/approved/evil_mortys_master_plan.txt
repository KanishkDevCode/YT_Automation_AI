What if I told you that Evil Morty's entire existence is just one giant social experiment on Rick Sanchez? Think about it: he's been watching and manipulating his alternate self for years, trying to prove that morality is just a product of circumstance. That's not some wild speculation - we've seen him in "Rick Potion #9", when he basically created a reality where Rick was the main villain.

But here's the thing: Evil Morty didn't just stop at making Rick bad. He went all the way, creating an alternate timeline where Rick was essentially a monster, doing whatever it takes to survive. And then... he gave him a family. Yeah, you heard that right - Evil Morty basically created a twisted, dark mirror of the Pickle Rick reality we saw in "Pickle Rick".

So what's the point of all this? Is Evil Morty just trying to prove some existential theory about free will and determinism? Or is it something more sinister?
