The Council of Ricks is actually a galactic-scale pyramid scheme, and I've got the receipts.

From "Rick Potion #9," we know that every Rick must contribute 10% of their dimensional wealth to the council's coffers upon joining. Then, in "Close Rick-counters of the Rick Kind," it's revealed that these donations are ostensibly used for interdimensional aid and research – but only after a massive chunk is siphoned off as "operational costs."

The logical conclusion? The Council of Ricks is using its seemingly altruistic facade to extract wealth from its members, essentially skimming off the cosmic cream. And you thought it was just about sharing knowledge and saving dimensions... Wake up, Ricks.
