Morty's been right all along: the Council of Ricks is secretly manipulating the Multiverse through a complex web of Temporal Insurance Policies.

From "The Rickshank Rickdemption," we know that the Council has control over temporal anomalies, which they use to maintain order and stability across the multiverse. Meanwhile, in "Pickle Rick," Morty discovers that his grandfather's actions have unintended consequences on the timeline, causing reality distortions.

It's clear: the Council is using these policies to absorb and mitigate the costs of their own reckless interventions, ensuring their own power while leaving innocent multiverses to deal with the fallout. Don't pretend you didn't see this coming; it's exactly what they do in the shadows – or rather, in the timelines where the risks are too great for even them to bear.
